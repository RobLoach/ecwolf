Use this Tab to enable and configure your Gamepad OR Keyboard.

1. Enable the Game Pad check box.
2. Hide Touch Controls if needed.
3. Press on an Action to configure.
4. Move or Press the Gamepad button.

Long press on an analog Action to set the sensitivity and invert.

The Actions with a joystick icon are for analog inputs ONLY. The other Actions can be set with gamepad buttons OR analog inputs.

Press the Back Button to cancel and clear an Action

MOGA Pocket:
Ensure your MOGA Pocket device is first connected via bluetooth to your Android device.

MOGA Pro:
You can set your game pad to A OR B mode, ensure it is first connected via bluetooth.
